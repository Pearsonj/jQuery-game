How To play!

Hello and Welcome to the Rupee Game! So here is how you play! At the beginning of each round a random number will be displayed on the screen in the box labled, " Try to match this score!" and all you have to do is match that score! To begin adding points to your accumulating score all you have to do is click on the Rupees below! Its up to you to figure out what each one is worth! So go forth and add up some points and Remember! Don't go over! or you lose!
